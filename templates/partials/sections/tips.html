Top 3 tips for forming habits

Tips that will help you stay on the path of forming new habits. Start small, be persistent - and the result will not make you wait!

01.

Start small

5 minutes a day is already a success. The main thing is regularity .

02.

Make a habit a routine

For example: "After breakfast - 5 minutes of English" .

03.

Mark progress

A habit tracker is a great way to see your results .
